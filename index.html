About Me

At the tender age of 15, I embarked on an electrifying journey into the realm of experimental electronic music. Fuelled by a passion that knew no bounds, I harnessed the raw energy of analog guitar and the boundless possibilities of digital synthesizers to sculpt a sonic universe uniquely my own.

By the time I turned 16, I birthed my inaugural album, a testament to the fusion of youthful curiosity and artistic fervor. The canvas of my compositions paints landscapes that oscillate between darkness and nostalgia, a sonic odyssey that invites listeners to explore the depths of emotion.

In my world, analog warmth collides with digital precision, giving rise to soundscapes that echo the whispers of introspection. Each beat, each note, is a reflection of my personal journey, a musical autobiography that unfolds with every nuanced melody.

Join me in this sonic expedition, where experimentation meets emotion, and the boundaries of conventional music dissolve into an atmospheric symphony. Together, let's traverse the uncharted territories of sound, weaving tales that resonate with the soul and linger in the recesses of memory.

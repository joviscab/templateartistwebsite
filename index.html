About

Began experimenting with sound at 15 — fusing analog guitar with digital synthesis to sculpt atmospheres that live between darkness and nostalgia.

By 16, released a debut album born from curiosity and raw sonic exploration. Each release since has been a journey deeper into the intersection of organic texture and electronic architecture.

The music draws from abandoned places, forgotten memories, and the quiet persistence of nature reclaiming what was lost. Analog warmth meets digital precision — introspection rendered in frequency.
